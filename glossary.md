# Glossary

- `Category code`: an existing Netflix genre or category code.
- `Canonical record`: the normalized, user-facing version of a category or taxonomy item.
- `Raw source record`: the stored original or near-original external ingestion payload with provenance.
- `Ingestion`: scheduled fetch, normalization, and refresh of approved source data.
- `Editorial recommendation`: a curated recommendation chosen or organized intentionally, not generated from personal behavior.
- `Rule-based recommendation`: a recommendation generated from tags, filters, and explicit matching rules.
- `Region-aware`: best-effort signals related to country or regional relevance, not guaranteed live availability truth.
- `Direct-open`: a link or flow that attempts to open Netflix at a relevant destination.
